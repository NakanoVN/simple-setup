### alpine doas troubleshooting

1. remove /etc/doas.d/*.conf
-- 'doas rm -rf /etc/doas.d/*.conf'

2. edit '/etc/doas.conf'
-- 'doas nvim /etc/doas.conf'

-- add this line:
permit <user> as root
permit nopass :wheel cmd reboot
permit nopass :wheel cmd poweroff
permit nopass :wheel cmd zzz
permit nopass :wheel cmd sh args -c "sleep 1 && zzz"
